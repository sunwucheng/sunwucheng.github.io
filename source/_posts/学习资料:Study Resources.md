- FEM: 有限单元法

- Zienkiewicz《The Finite Element Method for Solid and Structural Mechanics》 ★★★★
- Simo&Hughes 《Computational Inelasticity》 (Nonlinear) ★★★
- Ted Belytschko 《Nonlinear Finite Elements for Continua and Structures》 (Nonlinear) ★★★
